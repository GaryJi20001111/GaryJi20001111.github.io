Projects

  • "Study on the Time-Varying Property of Herd Behavior in China's Stock" ‒ December 2021 to June 2022
    Undergraduate Thesis (with English abstract)

    Established a dynamic model to test the significance and asymmetric characteristics of herd behavior in A-share market and Hong Kong stock market under different regimes based on CSAD method and Markov Switching model.

  • "Predict the Spread and Buzz about Asian Giant Hornets in Washington based on VAR and DCNN" ‒ February 2021
    2021 International Mathematical Contest in Modeling (team of 3)

    Built a vector autoregression model for predicting the spread of Asian giant hornets on 4440 reported sightings; created a convolutional neural network for detecting erroneously reported sightings, with BERT implemented to generate features from sighting records and transfer learning applied to preprocess imbalanced data.

  • "E. T. Fund: a Quantitative Private Equity Fund Based on Option Delivery Mechanism" ‒ September 2020 to March 2021
    Citi Cup Financial Innovation Application Competition (team of 10)

    Designed and optimized the arbitrage strategy for Shanghai Stock Exchange 50ETF options; built and integrate the alpha, risk and transaction cost models; enhanced trading signals in back-testing.

  • "Analysis of Teaming Strategies Based on Adjusted Passing Network and MLRM" ‒ February 2020
    2020 International Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling (team of 3)

    Created a network to identify team chemicals for each game with the Floyd-Warshall algorithm and the clustering model; constructed a regression model to identify performance indicators that reflect successful teamwork and conducted sensitivity analysis; applied a game theory model to analyze the strategy selection for the coach based on the pure and mixed Nash equilibrium.
